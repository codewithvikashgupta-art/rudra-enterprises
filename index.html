Who We Are

Quality Grain Milling From the Heart of Bihar

Rudra Enterprises is a Micro manufacturing enterprise operating from Dighiyar, Darbhanga, Bihar. We are engaged in the manufacture of grain mill products including flour milling (NIC 10611), serving local and regional markets with consistent quality.

Our enterprise is officially registered with the Government of India's Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises under the Udyam scheme, ensuring full regulatory compliance and quality accountability.

What We Make

Our Products

We specialise in grain processing and flour milling, providing quality products for household and commercial use.

Wheat Flour (Atta)

Freshly milled whole wheat flour with retained nutrients. Ideal for chapati, roti, and all daily cooking needs.

Grain Mill Products

Custom milling of various grains including maize, rice, and pulses — processed with care for purity and consistency.

Refined Flour (Maida)

Finely milled refined flour suitable for bakery, snacks, and food processing applications.
